## How to Use This Swipe File

Each template follows the **P.A.I.N.T. Framework**:
- **P**ersonalize — Research the prospect, reference something specific
- **A**gitate — Name the pain point they're feeling right now
- **I**magine — Paint a picture of the better way
- **N**ext Step — Low-friction, specific ask
- **T**rust — Social proof, case study, or credential

## Subject Line Formulas

### Formula 1: The Specific Result
> "[Company] → [Metric]% [Improvement] in [Timeframe]"
> *Example: "Wilson Realty → 40% more leads in 60 days"*

### Formula 2: The Mutual Connection
> "Quick question re: [Something on their website/blog]"
> *Example: "Quick question re: your recent merger announcement"*

### Formula 3: The Insight Hook
> "Saw your [Product/Feature] — have you considered [Alternative]?"
> *Example: "Saw your intake process — have you considered AI triage?"*

### Formula 4: The Pegged Event
> "[Event/News] + [Company] = [Opportunity]"
> *Example: "New GDPR rules + your data pipeline = compliance risk?"*

### Formula 5: The Curiosity Gap
> "An idea for [First Name]'s [Specific Role/Domain]"
> *Example: "An idea for Sarah's lead qualification pipeline"*

## Follow-Up Sequences

### Sequence A: The Value-Add (3 emails over 14 days)

**Follow-Up 1 (Day 3):** Share a relevant case study or result
> "Hi [First Name], thought you might find this useful — here's how [Similar Company] achieved [Result] with a similar approach. No pitch, just sharing."

**Follow-Up 2 (Day 7):** Offer a specific insight about their business
> "Hi [First Name], I noticed [specific observation about their website/product]. Did you know [related stat]? Happy to share how others are solving this."

**Follow-Up 3 (Day 14):** The soft break-up
> "Hi [First Name], I'll leave you with this — if [specific pain point] ever becomes a priority, we have a solution that's helped [X] companies. My calendar's open whenever you're ready."

### Sequence B: The Event-Triggered (tied to company milestones)

**Trigger:** New funding round, product launch, hiring spree, quarterly results
> "Hi [First Name], congrats on [event]! I reached out a few weeks ago about [topic]. With [event], [specific opportunity] might be even more relevant now. Worth a quick chat?"

### Sequence C: The Referral Angle (for warm introductions)

> "Hi [First Name], I know you might not be the right person for this, but who on your team owns [specific area]? I'd love to share what we've done for [Similar Company] with them."
